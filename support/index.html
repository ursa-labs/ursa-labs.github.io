Supporting Ursa Labs



Thank you for your interest in Ursa Labs. We depend on external financial support to provide a livelihood for software engineers creating open source software for the public good.

We are looking to build relationships with organizations which can provide either:

  • Direct financial support, which will be used to provide salary and benefits for software engineers working in the lab
  • Engineering collaborations on our roadmap

To contact us about ways to support Ursa Labs, please email us at info@ursalabs.org.
